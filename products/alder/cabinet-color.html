Cabinet Color Grade Alder Hardwood Lumber

Clear Cutting Boards
Two-Face cutting (CF2)

Cabinet Color is a high-grade product with heavy discoloration of white blotchy sticker impression or blue stain in the clear face cuttings. It was developed to maximize yields of clear two-face (C2F) cuttings in 24 inch (60.96 cm) to 56 inch (142.24 cm) lengths, with minimal development of cuttings below 24 inches (60.96 cm).
